**SKU:** WTP-002

## Product Description

Introducing the world's first toaster designed exclusively for pets! The WiFi-Enabled
Toaster for Pets allows your furry companions to enjoy perfectly warmed treats at
the touch of a paw. Connects to your home network for remote treat dispensing and
temperature monitoring via our PetToast app.

## Key Features

- **Paw-Activated Controls**: Large touch-sensitive buttons for easy pet operation
- **Species-Specific Settings**: Pre-programmed for dogs, cats, hamsters, and birds
- **Safety Lock**: Prevents accidental activation (or intentional by clever pets)
- **Treat Warming**: Gentle warming cycle (not actual toasting - that's dangerous)
- **WiFi Connectivity**: Monitor and control from anywhere
- **Camera Built-in**: Watch your pet enjoy their treat remotely
